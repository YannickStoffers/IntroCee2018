Introduction

Welcome to the wonderfull website of the Introduction Committee of 2018 of Cover. Are you starting the Bachelor Artificial Intelligence or Computing Science at the University of Groningen in 2018? Join the introduction camp organised by study association Cover! This is the opportunity to get to know your fellow students in a fun way and to make some new friends. We, the Introduction Committee, are really proud to organise this year's camp for you from the 8th till the 10th of September.

Throughout the entire weekend there will be a fun programme full of nice activities to meet a lot of new people (both first years and a bunch of senior students). What's more is that there is plenty of time to do just about anything else and have fun with your new friends or play some cards. In other words, spending the first weekend as a student at this camp, (with your fellow first years and a some seniors) is the best way to get a head start in your new life as a student!

Theme

The theme of this year's introduction camp is "Retro & Nostalgia". During the weekend you will delve into your (childhood) memories and relive all those moments that you have cherised for years. You will meet every character from your youth, from Spongebob to Mr. Krabs, from Simba to Scar, and so forth.

Location

This introductory camp will take place at a well-suited accommodation called Pagedal in the tiny town near Groningen called Stadskanaal, which itself is situated at the border between Groningen and Drenthe. Our departure from the university will be on Friday afternoon at 15:00, at which time a bus will take us straight to the campsite. As we made sure that you are completely free that Friday afternoon, you won't have to worry about missing any classes. Sometime on Sunday afternoon, after all the fun is said and done, we will travel back to Groningen Central Station or Zernike Campus and we hope you will take some amazing memories with you.
